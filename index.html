About

Marlboro is one of the world's most iconic and recognizable brands, synonymous with quality and tradition. Established in the 1920s, Marlboro has a rich history rooted in craftsmanship and excellence. From our beginnings as a small cigarette company, we have grown to become a global leader in the tobacco industry. Our commitment to innovation, combined with our dedication to maintaining the highest standards, has allowed Marlboro to stand the test of time. We pride ourselves on offering a distinctive smoking experience that reflects our heritage while embracing the future. At Marlboro, we believe in more than just delivering a product.
